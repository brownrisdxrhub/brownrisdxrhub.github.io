Brown | RISD XR HUB

What is XR?

XR stands for extended-reality, which is used as an umbrella term that groups augmented reality, virtual reality, and mixed-reality technologies together.

Our Mission

XR Hub seeks to provide an educational platform for Brown and RISD student bodies highlighting the rapidly-progressing world of XR technologies.

Members can work towards building the skills needed to create XR projects, ranging from games to art installations to public health initiatives and many other applications. As we move forward, our team will look for sponsorship, collaboration opportunities, and access to equipment both inside and outside the school.
