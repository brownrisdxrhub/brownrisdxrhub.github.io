About Us

We are a student-lead group with a mission to explore VR and AR technologies with other like-minded peers. Regardless of your experience with this technology, we welcome you to come explore our headsets, interact with existing applications, and learn to create your own XR experiences!

Exploring XR

The main priority of this club is to have fun with XR equipment! While XR is more accessible now than ever before, many individuals still haven‚Äôt tried XR equipment, and we hope to bridge that gap for students at Brown.

Education on how to create XR and what XR is

This semester, we will focus on equipping our general body members with the skills to create VR projects. In the future, we envision and are planning for field trips, guest speakers, collaborations with other clubs, and interactions with new equipment.

Creating XR projects
